About VIVA

Patient instructors. Real roads. Genuine confidence.

VIVA Driving School GB has been helping learners across North London pass their practical test and become safe, confident drivers for life. Every lesson is one-to-one, paced to how you learn, and built around the routes and junctions you'll actually be tested on.

Whether you're starting from zero, brushing up after a break, or pushing for a test date that's coming up fast, we'll build a plan that fits your goals — manual or automatic.
